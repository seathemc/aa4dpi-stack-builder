# 🇿🇲 Zambia: Land, forest, and climate records

Zambia: Land and forest records can support planning, traceability, climate finance, and community trust without creating another silo.

## Problem

Land, forest, community, and climate records are often separated, making it hard to verify tenure, permits, provenance, or climate-finance eligibility.

## Outcome

Land and forest records can support planning, traceability, climate finance, and community trust without creating another silo.
